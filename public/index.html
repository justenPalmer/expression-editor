Use the form to assist in input and modifying MapboxGL expressions. Check out the documentation for MapboxGL expressions here.

  • [ - start an expression
  • enter - update expression and add next element. Press enter on empty field to move up and add next.
  • backspace - back out of a field to remove it.

Also, you may paste in any MapboxGL expression into the bottom textarea and the form will build itself out to reflect the structure.
